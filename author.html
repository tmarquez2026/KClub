About the Authors

Hi, welcome to our version of KClub!! We wanted to see how KHub would be designed under our vision. Instantly, we thought dark mode because of course we would (be one with the dark side). After much thought, we also realized seeing is an important part of being productive so begrudgingly we added some lights. Neon lights !! To give an easy on the eyes glow in the generally dark background so we can remain comfortably within the dark. KClub comes from this because instead of knowledge hub its knowledge club, club because of the neon lights. Anyways, meet the team…
